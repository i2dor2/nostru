Presenting Nostru - a Chrome extension that maps your Nostr key to a Bitcoin Silent Payment address. Your npub becomes your sp1 address. Your nsec scans for incoming payments - locally, via a native host. The scan key never touches a remote server.

In NSP, scan_priv is root-equivalent to nsec. That's what @trbouma's brief makes explicit, and it's the constraint the whole architecture is built around.
